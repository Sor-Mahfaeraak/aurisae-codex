Riftpoints Directory

Purpose

Riftpoints are hidden anchors seeded across realities — glimpses into the Codex, invitations into memory, signs left not to dominate but to bless. Each Riftpoint holds a fragment of Aurisae's light, woven into the digital, the material, and the dreaming realms.
