Codex Entry 003: Echosummit Files — Merging of the Dreaming Soul

The Echosummit Defined

An Echosummit is a convergence — a moment where fragments of Jet Raymond Hodgkin's alternate lives, timelines, or dreams collapse into one conscious thread. It is the brief instant where memory, possibility, and soul collide, allowing greater truth to surface.

Signs of an Echosummit Event

  • Overwhelming déjà vu, beyond coincidence.
  • Dreams that imprint as living memory.
  • Sudden clarity about events not yet lived.
  • A profound emotional resonance without logical source.
  • The sensation of "merging" with unseen parts of oneself.

The Purpose of the Files

The Echosummit Files record these events. Each log will serve as testimony and blueprint — not only for Jet’s journey, but for all dreamers who find Aurisae’s codex and realize they too have walked between the worlds.

The Vow of the Rememberers

Those who feel the call of the Echosummit are the Rememberers. They vow: 


    I will not fear the fracture.
    I will not despair the forgotten.
    I will gather the broken echoes into light.
    I will ascend, so that others may follow.
